Our AI Agent Programs

Go Beyond Automation. Build an Autonomous Workforce.

The revolution is here. We are moving from AI that follows instructions to AI that takes initiative. AI Agents are autonomous systems that can perceive their environment, make decisions, and take actions to achieve complex goals. At LifeGenBio, we teach you how to build these digital employees, transforming you from a user of AI into an architect of intelligent, autonomous solutions that will define the next decade.

Meet the Future

Workshops & Seminars

Step into the world of agentic AI. Our introductory workshops are designed to give you a thrilling glimpse into the future of work and technology. Understand what makes an AI an "agent," explore the groundbreaking potential of autonomous systems, and witness live demos of agents performing tasks like research, coding, and complex problem-solving. This is your gateway to the most exciting frontier in artificial intelligence.

The Agentic Shift: Understand the difference between chatbots and true AI agents.

Core Components: Learn about planning, memory, and tool usage in agents.

Meet the Frameworks: A high-level introduction to powerful libraries like LangChain & LlamaIndex.

Live Demonstrations: See AI agents in action, tackling real-world challenges autonomously.

From Prompt to Agent

Short-Term Agent Developer Course

Theory is exciting, but building is empowering. This intensive, hands-on course takes you from concept to creation in just a few weeks. You will learn to design, code, and deploy your first AI agents. Equip your agents with tools to browse the web, execute code, and interact with APIs. By the end of this course, you'll have a portfolio of functional AI agents and the foundational skills to build even more sophisticated solutions.

Agent Architectures: Master concepts like ReAct (Reason+Act) for robust agent design.

Practical Tool Building: Give your agents custom abilities to interact with any data source or API.

Memory Management: Implement short-term and long-term memory for context-aware agents.

Project-Based Learning: Build a research agent, a coding assistant, and a personal task manager.

Long-Term Multi-Agent Systems Program

Become an elite architect of complex AI ecosystems. This flagship program is for those who want to lead the agentic revolution. Move beyond single agents to orchestrating teams of specialized AI agents that collaborate to solve monumental tasks. Agent Security & Governance: Learn to build safe, reliable, and controllable autonomous systems.

Capstone Project: Design and deploy a sophisticated multi-agent solution for a real-world business problem.
