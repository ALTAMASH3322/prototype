Decoding Gene Regulation

Advanced Epigenetic Profiling

Our epigenetics services provide an in-depth analysis of epigenetic modifications, elucidating their crucial roles in gene regulation and complex disease mechanisms. We help you look beyond the DNA sequence to understand how gene expression is controlled.

By integrating Artificial Intelligence with advanced sequencing techniques, we offer comprehensive profiling of DNA methylation, histone modifications, and chromatin structure. This enables profound insights into cellular processes, uncovering novel therapeutic targets and biomarkers for precision medicine.
