Our Capabilities

A Spectrum of Omics Services

Genomics Services

Unlock the genetic blueprint. We provide comprehensive DNA sequencing and analysis to identify variations, reconstruct genomes, and develop genetic markers for applications in medicine, agriculture, and beyond.

Key Services:
  • Whole Genome & Exome Sequencing (WGS/WES)
  • Reference Guided Assembly & De Novo Assembly
  • Comparative & Functional Genomics
  • Variant Calling (SNPs, Indels, SVs)
  • Marker Development (QTLseq, GBS)

Transcriptomics Services

Capture a dynamic snapshot of gene activity. Our RNA-Seq services quantify expression levels, identify novel transcripts, and uncover regulatory networks that control cellular function in health and disease.

Key Services:
  • mRNA & Total RNA Sequencing
  • Differential Gene Expression (DGE) Analysis
  • Small RNA (miRNA) Sequencing and Analysis
  • Pathway and Gene Ontology (GO) Enrichment Analysis
  • Co-expression Network Analysis

Proteomics Services

Analyze the functional machinery of the cell. Using mass spectrometry-based approaches, we identify and quantify proteins and their post-translational modifications to reveal cellular states and signaling pathways.

Key Services:
  • Protein Identification and Quantification (LC-MS/MS)
  • Shotgun & Targeted Proteomics
  • Post-Translational Modification (PTM) Analysis
  • Protein-Protein Interaction (PPI) Network Analysis
  • Quantitative Proteomics (SILAC, TMT, iTRAQ)
