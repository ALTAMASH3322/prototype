FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Are these workshops beginner-friendly?

Most of our workshops are designed for beginners with no prior experience required, unless specified otherwise in the description. We start with the fundamentals and build up to the hands-on project.

What do I need to bring or prepare?

All you need is your own laptop and an eagerness to learn! We will send a detailed setup guide with any required software installations a week before the workshop date.

Will I receive a certificate after the workshop?

Yes, all attendees who successfully complete the workshop will receive a verifiable Certificate of Completion from LifeGenBio, which you can add to your LinkedIn profile and resume.

What is your cancellation policy?

You can receive a full refund if you cancel up to 7 days before the workshop date. A 50% refund is available for cancellations made up to 3 days prior. Unfortunately, we cannot offer refunds for cancellations made less than 72 hours before the event.
