Our Core Capabilities

A Spectrum of Metagenomic Services

Taxonomical Classification

Utilizing AI-driven algorithms and curated databases to accurately identify and classify microbial species (who is there?) within your samples.

Functional Classification

Uncovering the metabolic potential and functional roles (what are they doing?) of the microbial community by analyzing gene functions and pathways.

Metagenomics Analysis

Comprehensive end-to-end analysis from raw sequencing data to insightful biological conclusions, including alpha and beta diversity analysis.

Comparative Metagenomics & Meta-transcriptomics

Compare microbial communities across different conditions or time points to identify key differences. Analyze gene expression (meta-transcriptomics) to understand active functions.

Integration of Taxonomical & Functional Data

Our AI models synthesize taxonomical and functional data to create a holistic view, linking specific microbes to critical metabolic functions and ecological roles.
