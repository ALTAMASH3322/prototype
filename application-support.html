Our Service

Ensuring Your Software Runs Flawlessly

Launching an application is just the beginning. To ensure business continuity and keep your users happy, you need reliable, responsive, and expert support. LifeGenBio's Application Support services act as an extension of your team, providing the technical expertise to resolve issues quickly, perform critical maintenance, and ensure your software delivers consistent value long after deployment.

24/7 Helpdesk & L1 Support

We provide the first line of defense for your users, offering immediate assistance, logging tickets, and resolving common issues to ensure a positive user experience around the clock.

L2 & L3 Technical Support

Our expert engineers handle complex technical challenges, performing deep-dive troubleshooting, root cause analysis, and bug fixes to resolve incidents efficiently.

Proactive Application Monitoring

We don't wait for things to break. Our team proactively monitors application health, performance, and uptime to identify and address potential issues before they impact your business.

Routine Maintenance & Updates

We handle all essential maintenance, including security patching, version updates, database optimization, and backups, to keep your application secure and performing optimally.

Bug Fixes & Patch Management

Our structured process ensures that bugs are logged, prioritized, and resolved efficiently. We manage the entire patch deployment lifecycle to minimize disruption.

Custom Service Level Agreements (SLAs)

We offer flexible support plans with guaranteed response and resolution times, providing you with the peace of mind and predictability your business requires.

Need a Reliable Support Partner?

Focus on your core business while we handle the health of your applications. Let's discuss a support plan that fits your needs and gives you the confidence that your software is always in good hands.
